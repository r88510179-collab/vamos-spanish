🇭🇳 What is ¡Vamos!?

A free 12-month Spanish learning program built for you and your toddler. You'll learn conversational Honduran Spanish together — just 45 minutes a day, broken into small chunks around naps and meals. No experience needed.
📱 What's inside?

- 48 weekly lessons that tell you exactly what to do step by step
- Flashcards with 120+ words and pronunciation audio
- Toddler Mode — hand your phone to your child and let them tap to learn
- Daily tasks to build a consistent habit
- Honduran culture guide — slang, food, music, recipes
- Fort Myers field trips — practice Spanish at local markets and parks
⏰ How much time does it take?

45 minutes a day, split up like this:
- Morning song + getting dressed (5 min)
- Meals — narrate in Spanish (5 min)
- Video together (10 min)
- Play activity (10 min)
- Solo study during nap time (15 min)
- Bedtime routine in Spanish (5 min)

5 out of 7 days is success. No perfection needed.
💾 Does it save my progress?

Yes — automatically. Everything saves to your phone. Add this to your home screen for the best experience (instructions are inside the app after setup).
